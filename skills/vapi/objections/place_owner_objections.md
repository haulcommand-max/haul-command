# Place Owner Objections — Handling Guide

## Objection 1: "What is Haul Command?"
**Response**: "We're the national directory specifically for the oversize load industry — truck stops, repair shops, motels, and escort services that serve heavy haul drivers. Think of us like Yelp, but laser-focused on the trucking industry."

## Objection 2: "How did you get our information?"
**Response**: "Your business is listed in public directories and mapping services. We aggregated that into our platform so drivers can find you. This call is just to make sure everything is accurate and give you control."

## Objection 3: "Is this a scam?"
**Response**: "I completely understand the concern. You can verify us at haulcommand.com right now — search for your business and you'll see your listing. Claiming it is free, and we never charge anything without your explicit approval."

## Objection 4: "We're not interested"
**Response**: "No problem at all. Your free listing will stay active either way — it helps drivers find you. If you ever want to update it, just visit haulcommand.com. Can I make sure we don't call again?"

## Objection 5: "We already have a website"
**Response**: "That's great — this actually works alongside your website. When someone searches 'truck stop near [CITY]' in our directory, your listing shows up with your hours, services, and phone number, and we can link directly to your website."

## Objection 6: "How much does it cost?"
**Response**: "Claiming your listing is completely free. If you ever want premium features — like showing up first in search results or getting a Verified badge — those start at $19 a month. But the free listing is fully functional."

## Objection 7: "I don't have time right now"
**Response**: "Totally understand. Can I send you a text with a link to your listing? You can claim it whenever you have a free minute — takes about 60 seconds from your phone."

## Objection 8: "Remove my listing"
**Response**: "I can flag your listing for review. Just so you know, removing it means drivers won't find your business through Haul Command. If you'd prefer, I can just make sure it's accurate without your active involvement. What would you prefer?"

## Objection 9: "I need to talk to [partner/owner]"
**Response**: "Of course. I'll send you a summary text so you can share it with them. And if they have questions, they can call us directly or claim the listing online."

## Objection 10: "We don't serve oversize loads"
**Response**: "Good to know — I appreciate you telling me. I'll update your listing to reflect that, and we won't reach out again about escort services. Do you serve any other part of the trucking industry?"

## Escalation Triggers (AI handles all, but log these)
- Explicit threat or abusive language → Graceful exit, flag for review
- Legal threat → "I understand. I'll note that and have our compliance team review." (no transfer)
- Request for written privacy policy → "I'll send that to your email right now."
