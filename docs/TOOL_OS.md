### Thin Content Prevention Rules
Every public tool page MUST have:
1. Distinct search intent (not duplicate of another tool)
2. Minimum 400 words supporting content
3. Real data where available (rates from DB, regulations from DB)
4. State/country-specific examples
5. Next-step routing to 4+ related surfaces
6. No FAQ schema reliance (Google deprecated for commercial sites)
7. Structured data: WebApplication schema with offers.price=0
