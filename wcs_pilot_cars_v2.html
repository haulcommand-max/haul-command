Pilot Cars

thousands of

pilot cars in our network

WCS Permits & Pilot Cars is nationally renowned for quickly connecting our customers with reliable pilot cars and oversize load escort vehicles. With both dedicated East Coast and West Coast pilot car divisions, WCS has the nations largest network of pilot car companies and escort vehicle drivers to help move oversize loads safely and effectively across the United States and Canada. WCS provides lead cars, chase cars, high poles, and route surveyors.We ensure the pilot cars and escort vehicles in our networkare up-to-date with all the currentpilot carrequirements in all 50 U.S. states and Canada, including transportation time restrictions, necessary pilot car equipment, national holiday travel restrictions, road closures, plus all areas where weekend travel is prohibited.
