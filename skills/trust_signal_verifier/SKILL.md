# Trust Signal Verifier

## Verification Targets

1. **Insurance validity** — Confirm insurance documents are current, not expired, and match the operator
2. **Equipment claims** — Cross-reference claimed equipment against photos, certifications, and job history
3. **Experience years** — Validate stated experience against account age, job history, and public records
4. **Location consistency** — Check that claimed service areas match actual activity patterns and GPS data
5. **Review authenticity** — Detect fake or incentivized reviews using velocity, language, and reviewer patterns

## Output

- **Trust confidence** — Overall trust score (0-100) based on verified vs. claimed signals
- **Fraud flags** — Specific inconsistencies or red flags requiring investigation
- **Verification tasks** — Ordered list of items that need manual or automated verification
