# The Perfect Posting Set: Social Media Grid

This document defines the exact posting recipes for each platform to ensure we are building Habit and Authority, not just "noise."

## 1. Facebook Group (The Engine Room)
- **Goal**: Community Lock-in & Marketplace Trust.
- **Frequency**: 3-5x per day.
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Post 1 (Morning)**: "Load Quality Check" — A binary question about a current industry rate or move.
    - **Post 2 (Mid-day)**: "Value Bomb" — A 1-minute snippet from the HeyGen Rail (e.g., "The Dispatch Trap").
    - **Post 3 (Afternoon)**: "Member Spotlight" — Highlighting a verified operator's clean set-up (Visual proof of quality).
    - **Post 4 (Evening)**: "The Standard Audit" — A poll regarding a specific broker's payment or safety cult reputation.
- **CTA**: Link to the private **Reliability Standard** submission funnel.

## 2. Facebook Page (The SEO Funnel)
- **Goal**: Google Discovery & SEO Crawlability.
- **Frequency**: 1x per day.
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Post**: A long-form "Authority Guide" summary with a high-res graphic of an oversize move.
- **CTA**: Direct link to the **FB Group** (Discovery ➡️ Engagement).

## 3. LinkedIn (The Professional Identity)
- **Goal**: Broker Trust & Executive Outreach.
- **Frequency**: 3x per week.
- **Posting Set**:
    - **The Content**: "Industry Intelligence Reports" — PDF carousels or long-form articles on billions in upcoming infrastructure.
- **CTA**: Direct contact for "Command Suite" enterprise demos.

## 4. YouTube / TikTok (The Authority Pusher)
- **Goal**: Video SEO & Mass Awareness.
- **Frequency**: 1x long-form (YouTube), 3x Shorts recurring.
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Long-form**: HeyGen 10-minute deep-dives (e.g., "Solar Farm Logistics").
    - **Shorts**: "10-Question Pre-Move Checklist" snippets.
- **CTA**: Link to the **"Where The Money Is"** report lead magnet.

## 5. Reddit / Industry Forums (The Debate Floor)
- **Goal**: Community Authority & Backlink Gravity.
- **Frequency**: Daily (Reactive/Proactive).
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Action**: Participating in "r/Truckers" or specific pilot car subreddits with source-backed regulatory data.
- **CTA**: Minimal. Act as the "Source of Truth" to drive brand searches.

## 6. Pinterest (The Visual SEO Secret)
- **Goal**: Visual Search Traffic.
- **Frequency**: 5+ Pins per day (Automated).
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Format**: "Equipment Mounting Cards" and "State Spec Signs" as visual infographics.
- **CTA**: Link to the **Fulfillment Rail** (Store) to buy the physical signs/gear.

## 8. X / Twitter (The Velocity Layer)
- **Goal**: Real-time traffic alerts & Industry Gossip.
- **Frequency**: 5-10x per day (High Velocity).
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Content**: Rapid-fire alerts on bridge strikes, closed routes, and immediate permit delays.
- **CTA**: Link to the **"Regulatory Knowledge Graph"** real-time alert feed.

## 9. Quora (The Intent Layer)
- **Goal**: Capturing "High-Intent" Questions.
- **Frequency**: Weekly (Highly targeted).
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Action**: Answering specific questions like "How do I become a pilot car driver?" with 2,000-word authoritative answers.
- **CTA**: Link to the **"New Escort Starter Kit"** (Gravity Pocket lead magnet).

## 10. Tumblr (The Niche SEO Layer)
- **Goal**: Long-tail Imagery & Backlink Stacking.
- **Frequency**: Automated (Repurposed from Pinterest).
- **Posting Set**:
    - **Content**: Aesthetic "Heavy Haul" photography and equipment diagrams.
- **CTA**: Passive branding. Use it for the "Entity Stack" to boost the main site's DA.

---
**The "Loop" Strategy**: We post to the **FB Page** primarily to let Google "see" the engagement that is happening inside the private **FB Group**. Page = Discovery, Group = Engagement.
