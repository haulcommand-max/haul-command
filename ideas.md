# 5 Content Ideas for Oversize Haul & Escort Business

1.  **"The Route Recap" (Time-lapse)**
    *   **Concept:** A sped-up POV video from the dashcam of a pilot car or the hauler itself, showing a particularly narrow bridge crossing or tight turn.
    *   **Hook:** "Think your commute is hard? Try squeezing a 14-foot wide load through this."

2.  **"Pre-Trip Perfection" (Educational)**
    *   **Concept:** A quick checklist style video showing the specific safety flags, banners, and light checks required before the wheels turn.
    *   **Hook:** "Safety isn't an accident. Here's our 5-minute ritual before every haul."

3.  **"The Monster Load Showcase" (Visual/Status)**
    *   **Concept:** High-quality photos or drone shots of the most impressive, heaviest, or widest load of the week. specific stats (weight, width, destination).
    *   **Hook:** "120,000 lbs. 16 feet wide. Moving mountains today."

4.  **"Escort Driver Confidential" (Q&A/Humor)**
    *   **Concept:** A pilot car driver explaining the weirdest things they see drivers do around wide loads, or explaining what the pole on the front of the car actually does.
    *   **Hook:** "Stop cutting us off! Here is what happens when you tailgate a wide load."

5.  **"The Regulations Minute" (Industry Authority)**
    *   **Concept:** Bite-sized tips explaining complex state-to-state permit differences or curfew rules for wide loads.
    *   **Hook:** "Why we had to park at 2 PM in Florida but could run all night in Texas."
