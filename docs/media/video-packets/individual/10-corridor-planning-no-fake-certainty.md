Hook: A corridor page should help you plan. It should not pretend every rate, provider, or restriction is guaranteed.

Script:
1. Good corridor planning connects route context, support roles, requirements, tools, and next actions.
1. Bad corridor content pretends it knows more than the data proves.
1. Haul Command should show source confidence, support gaps, provider paths, and where to verify before dispatch.
1. That is how corridor intelligence becomes useful instead of just decorative.
1. Use the corridor page to plan the support stack, then post the load or find providers.
