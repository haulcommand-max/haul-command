# Operator Opportunity Radar

## Ranking Factors

1. **Nearby load density** — Volume of open loads within the operator's active service area
2. **Match likelihood** — Probability of being selected based on equipment, experience, and reputation
3. **Revenue potential** — Estimated earnings for each opportunity
4. **Competition level** — Number of other qualified operators competing for the same load
5. **Timing window** — Urgency and deadline proximity of available loads

## Output

- **Top opportunities** — Ranked list of the 5-10 best-fit loads for the operator
- **Win probability** — Percentage likelihood of being selected for each opportunity
- **Suggested actions** — Specific steps to improve chances (update availability, adjust rate, expand corridor coverage)
