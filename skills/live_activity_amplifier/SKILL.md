# Live Activity Amplifier

## Events to Surface

1. **New loads posted** — Fresh load opportunities entering the marketplace
2. **Operators going available** — Operators updating their status to available or active
3. **Reviews submitted** — New reviews and ratings from brokers
4. **Corridor surges** — Demand spikes detected in specific corridors
5. **Profile upgrades** — Operators completing verifications, adding insurance, or claiming profiles

## Rules

- **Prioritize high-value events** — Surface events that demonstrate marketplace health and urgency
- **Avoid spam clustering** — Enforce minimum spacing between similar event types in the feed
- **Maintain geographic relevance** — Prioritize events near the viewer's location or active corridors
