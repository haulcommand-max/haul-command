# SEO Surface Expander

## Discovery Model

1. **Keyword gaps** — Identify high-volume search terms with no existing landing page
2. **Corridor combinations** — Generate pages for origin-destination pairs (e.g., "Houston to Dallas heavy haul escort")
3. **Equipment niches** — Create pages for specific equipment categories (lowboy, beam, transformer)
4. **Near-me variants** — Build localized "near me" and city-specific pages
5. **Permit/authority pages** — Generate compliance-focused pages per state/country

## Output

- **Page opportunities** — List of recommended new SEO surfaces with target keywords
- **Priority score** — Ranked by estimated search volume × conversion potential × competition level
- **Build queue** — Ordered backlog ready for the programmatic page generator
