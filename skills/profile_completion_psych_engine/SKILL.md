# Profile Completion Psychology Engine

## Mechanisms

1. **Progress milestones** — Trigger celebrations and unlocks at 10%, 30%, 60%, 90% completion
2. **Urgency prompts** — Time-limited nudges ("Complete your profile this week to appear in corridor searches")
3. **Social proof nudges** — Show what top-ranked operators have completed ("87% of verified operators have added insurance docs")
4. **Unlock rewards** — Gate premium features behind completion thresholds (lead routing at 60%, priority placement at 90%)
5. **Visibility incentives** — Show estimated ranking boost for each completed section

## Output

- **Completion delta** — Percentage improvement after nudge cycle
- **Next best action** — The single highest-impact field or section to complete next
- **Nudge message** — Copy-ready message for in-app notification, email, or SMS
